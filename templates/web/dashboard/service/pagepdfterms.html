Cancellation and Refund Policy

Please find the cancellation policy below. Please note that NO Cancellation requests will be taken on Sundays, Public Holidays and Cancellation done after office hours. In Case a cancellation request is send after office hours then it will be considered in the next day.

  • 50% Cancellation fee will be charged for cancellation received less than 7 days before the arrival date.
  • 75% Cancellation fee will be charged for cancellation received less than 120 Hours before the arrival time.
  • No refund for cancellation received before 72 Hours before the arrival time.

For Trips/Activities1. Full refund upon cancellation received before 10 Days of arrival (Terms and conditions applicable as per the criteria)

  • 50% Cancellation fee will be charged for cancellation received less than 7 Days before the arrival date.
  • 75% Cancellation fee will be charged for cancellation received less than 120 Hours before the arrival time.
  • No refund for cancellation received before 72 Hours before the arrival time.
  • 500 Rupees will be charged per cancellation of the ticket.

For Hotels Cancellation as per hotel policy, we do not charge any extra. Usually the hotel cancellation policy at the islands is as under:

  • If cancelled from 0 days to 7 days before the check-in date 100% of the booking amount will be charged
  • If cancelled from 7 days to 20 days before the check-in date 50% of the booking amount will be charged
  • If cancelled from 20 days to 30 days before the check-in date 25% of the booking amount will be charged
  • 500 Rupees will be charged per cancellation of the ticket.
  • If cancelled from 30 days or more before the check-in date 15% of the booking amount will be charged
  • No refund for cancellation received on bookings from 15th Dec to 15th Jan
  • No refunds for unused nights or early check-out
  • The Hotel is not responsible for room cancellation for the cancellation of flight / ferry
  • The Hotel is not responsible for cancellation of reservation due to climatic changes

    • Peak Time in the Andaman Islands there is a peak season time from 1-December to 31-January. During this time NO Cancellations are allowed. This is applicable on all hotels, activities, ferry and transport. We know that this sounds absurd, however due to high influx of travelers and very minimal availability of Hotels, Activities, transport and ferry such measures are taken. Thank you for the cooperation.

For Packages with Flight for our limited-edition tour packages with flight

  • No Refund if the flight gets cancelled / Delayed /Rescheduled
  • No refund for cancellation of the flight tickets at any time after the booking is made
  • No refund for the trip due to delay/cancellation/rescheduled in the flight timings, however if the guest manages to come at his own cost then the trip could be done as per the itinerary agreed upon. However, no refund shall be given for the day missed

For All Other Travel Packages (Honeymoon, Adventure, Family and Customized)

  • Cancellation Charge of Rupees 3500/- Per
  • Person will be charged at any time after the Booking is confirmed
  • No refund, if Cancellation received less than 10 Days before arrival
  • 75% of the total package amount will be charged, if Cancellation received between 11 - 20 Days before arrival
  • 50% of the total package amount will be charged, if Cancellation received between 21 - 30 Days before arrival be considered in the next day
